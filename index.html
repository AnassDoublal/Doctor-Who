The Series

The adventures of a Time Lord, one of a race of humanoid alien beings from the planet Galifrey. His/her name has never been revealed; he/she is known only as "The Doctor". He/she travels through time and space in a "TARDIS", which stands for "Time And Relative Dimensions In Space". It is much bigger inside than outside. In his/her travels, he encounters various monsters such as the Daleks, the Cybermen, and a renegade Time Lord known only as the Master. When the Doctor faces death, he/she cheats it by literally becoming a new person.
